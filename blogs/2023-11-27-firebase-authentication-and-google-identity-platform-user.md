Firebase Authentication and Google Identity Platform are the two Google products that offer identity management. Both products suffer from a vulnerability that allows an attacker to enumerate users on an application powered by Firebase Authentication or Google Identity Platform. In this article, we’ll show how this vulnerability can be used and what the impact is.
